About Me

I am a Web Developer based in Orange County. Growing up I always love bringing creative ideas to reality, either through drawing or sculpting. I eventually pursued an art career as a 3d modeler. While I was studying 3d modeling, I started to learn python and mel scripts in order to make my modeling easier and more efficient.

I got interested in how these scripts were made and tinkering with the code lead to me to path of being a programmer. Programming became as exciting as art to me. To see my code develop into something visually or physically gives me great joy.I am excited to grow and learn on my journey as a Web Developer. I hope to continue to make apps that would better our lives.

Aside from programming, I'm a nerd when it comes to Magic the Gathering.

Posterizer Online Shopping Store

Posterizer is a full-stack store demo that sells posters. The app has features of an online store, checking out poster details, adding posters to the shopping cart, removing cart items, choosing the quantity of posters, and checking out the cart.

I used React.js, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, React, Bootstrap, PHP, MySQL, phpMyAdmin

Live Demo GitHub

Melleau

Melleau is a mood tracker app. It was created as a group project. Its purpose is to provide users a means to keep track of their emotional health. I was a full stack developer with a focus on the front end in this project.

I used React.js, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, React, Bootstrap, PHP, MySQL, phpMyAdmin, Photoshop
